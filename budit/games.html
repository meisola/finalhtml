SodaSoda Shoot

shooting game

Overview

Brief: A RPG shooting game which can make players relax and work together. The goal of the game is to beat the monsters by guns and some tools. The differnt is that the weapons in this game are food, so it can make player relax and pleased.

My role: Game's UX, 3D modeling, character design.

Purpose Make a game which can work together, and relax.

Process

This is our graduation project. We first made the plan, including playing mode, game style and did some competitive analysis. At the same time we did the research about user experience of games. Then we started to design and code.

Research

We first looked into our experience and focus on shooting games system.

Target Audience


People aged 10 to 30 who want to play games together, and like shooting games.

Introduce


We spent one year to make this RPG shooting game.It can play with your friends to fight the monster with gun, and used some means to clear. In this game all of the bullets are soda, and that is the reason why this game called sodasoda shoot.
