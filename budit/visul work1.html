Overview

Brief : This is a project in collaboration between a publishing company and a tutoring center.

My role : UI design、HTML

Difficult : This is a project primarily focused on collaboration between two companies. Therefore, it's essential to meet the requirements and designs needed by both parties. Bicycle company project

Brief : This project is primarily for a bicycle company that aims to create a web page to promote its products online.

My role : All the ui/ux design and HTML、CSS coding

Difficult : The main focus for the customer is to clearly communicate their products. Since the company's primary clientele consists of local senior citizens, usability takes precedence over a flashy design. So, apart from the initial company introduction, my emphasis is on presenting a clean and easily comprehensible product catalog.
